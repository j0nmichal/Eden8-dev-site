Our Mission

The world is splitting into builders and the displaced.

Aviation, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing are among the fastest-growing workforce sectors in the country — and among the most difficult to enter without early exposure. By the time most kids encounter these fields, they've already decided they don't belong in them.

Eden 8 exists to change that calculus. We build programs that meet students in Title 1 schools — in East Salinas, in Los Angeles County, in the communities where early STEAM access is rarest and the stakes of missing it are highest.

We are not building charity. We are building infrastructure. The kind that compounds.

Flagship Program

Mighty Tails — where aviation meets animation.

An original animated series and standards-aligned K–5 curriculum that introduces young learners to aviation, drone technology, and the science behind flight. Built for Title 1 classrooms. Designed to stick.
